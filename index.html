Readbubo ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by JJ Studios, based in Australia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Readbubo mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

JJ Studios
Australia

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive only your name and email address from those providers — we never receive or store your Apple or Google password.

2.2 Profile Information

Your chosen display name, selected learning language, and dictionary language preference.

2.3 Content You Import

Text files, EPUB files, and audio files you import into the App are stored on our servers so you can access them across sessions. You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the right to import and use any content. We do not review or screen imported content.

2.4 Learning & Usage Data

Reading progress, saved vocabulary, flashcard review history, translation history, and daily activity data. This data is the core of the service and is necessary to provide it.

2.5 Translation Requests

When you tap a word or phrase for translation, that text is sent to DeepL or Google Translate APIs to generate the translation. We do not store your translation requests beyond what is cached in your word list.

2.6 Audio Transcription

If you use the audio import feature, your audio file is sent to Groq's Whisper API for transcription. The transcribed text is then stored in your account. Audio files count toward your storage limit and are deleted when you remove the import or delete your account.

2.7 Subscription Information

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple (via the App Store). We receive your subscription status (active, expired, cancelled) via RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

2.8 Technical Data

Device type, operating system version, and app version, collected automatically to ensure compatibility and fix bugs. We do not use persistent advertising identifiers.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active and the service is in use.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account through the App, your personal data (account info, saved words, reading progress, flashcard data, activity history, and private imported texts) is permanently deleted from our systems. Audio files stored in our file storage are deleted immediately. Deletion is complete within 30 days.
  • Shared/public content: If you have published content to the shared Discover library, that content may be retained with the author attribution removed (your user ID is set to null). You may request full removal by contacting us.
  • Legal holds: We may retain certain data where required by law or to resolve disputes.
  • Translation requests: Text snippets sent to DeepL or Google Translate are not retained by us beyond your saved word list. Each provider's own retention policy governs data on their servers.

7.1 All Users

  • Access and correction: You can view and update your profile information within the App at any time.
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account from Settings → Delete Account. This permanently erases all your data.
  • Data export: You can export your vocabulary list as a CSV file from the Wordlist screen.

  • Data portability (Article 20) — vocabulary export is available; for full data portability requests contact us
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests (Article 21)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at readbuboapp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7.4 Australian Residents (Privacy Act 1988)

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to access and correct the personal information we hold about you, and to make a complaint if you believe we have handled your information incorrectly. Contact us at readbuboapp@gmail.com. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS/HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt via Supabase Auth — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on our database ensure each user can only access their own data
  • API keys and secrets are stored in environment variables, never in app code

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at readbuboapp@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Readbubo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us at readbuboapp@gmail.com.
