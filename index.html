Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Readbubo ("we", "our", or "us") is a language learning app that helps you read in a foreign language by providing inline word and phrase translations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth).

Reading content: Text and EPUB files you import are stored on our servers so you can access them across sessions. Audio files you import for transcription are stored temporarily and count toward your storage limit.

Usage data: We store your reading progress, saved words, flashcard review history, and translation history to provide the core features of the app.

Purchase information: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of your subscription status via RevenueCat but do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Readbubo service
  • To sync your reading progress and vocabulary across devices
  • To process and manage your subscription
  • To transcribe audio files you upload (processed via Groq's Whisper API)
  • To translate words and phrases (processed via DeepL and Google Translate APIs)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • DeepL — word and phrase translation
  • Google Translate — word and phrase translation
  • Groq (Whisper) — audio transcription

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing their use of data.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is deleted from our systems within 30 days. Audio files are deleted when you delete the associated import.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the email below. You can also delete your account directly from the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Readbubo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: readbuboapp@gmail.com
